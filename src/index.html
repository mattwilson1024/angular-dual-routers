This example is a hybrid app using both AngularJS (grey) and Angular (blue) components.

The "Movies" route is handled by Angular, while the "Actors" route is handled by AngularJS. The dotted lines show the two router outlets.

The links allow you to navigate between the two pages, which works correctly provided they are [routerLink] directives and not just normal hyperlinks.

Problem: If you start on the actors page and navigate to "#/movies" by changing the URL, the component will not load until you click on the page.
